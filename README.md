## Overview

**BioGlow Tiles™** are **living, self-powered lighting panels** that harness **engineered bioluminescent organisms** (safe, non-toxic strains of algae and fungi) to produce a **soft, ambient glow** without electricity, batteries, or solar charging.  

Designed for **everyday consumers**, they provide a **sustainable, beautiful, and cost-saving alternative** to outdoor and indoor lighting, while also functioning as **CO₂ absorbers** and contributors to **urban greening**.

---

## Core Features

- **Zero Power Required** – No batteries, no wiring, no solar panels.  
- **Natural Bioluminescence** – Soft glow that doesn’t disrupt wildlife (unlike LEDs).  
- **Carbon-Negative** – Each tile captures up to **0.5 kg of CO₂/year** during its lifecycle.  
- **Long-Lasting Glow** – Operates for **6–12 months** before needing a bio-refill kit.  
- **Customizable Designs** – Available in multiple colors, patterns, and brightness levels.

---

## How It Works

1. **Bioluminescent Microorganisms** – Non-toxic algae and fungi housed in a nutrient gel.  
2. **Transparent Bioreactor Panel** – Protects and sustains organisms with air vents for gas exchange.  
3. **Self-Sustaining Cycle** – Organisms feed on **captured CO₂ and trace nutrients**, maintained via refill kits.  
4. **Natural Light Output** – Provides **20–60 lumens per tile**, ideal for pathways, patios, or mood lighting.

## Manufacturing & Scalability

- **Materials**:  
  - Recycled bioplastics for the casing.  
  - Nutrient gel (fully renewable and safe).  
  - Engineered, long-lived algae/fungal strains.

- **Production**:  
  - **Injection-molded and filled** in local or microfactories.  
  - Estimated **$5–$10 USD production cost per unit**.  
  - Retail-ready at **$20–$50 USD** (bulk décor packs available).

- **Scalability**:  
  - **Franchise-ready DAO model** enables local entrepreneurs to produce and distribute tiles.  
  - Shelf-stable before activation, easy to ship globally.

## Environmental Impact

- **Zero Energy Consumption** – Each tile replaces **~20 kWh/year** of electric lighting.  
- **CO₂ Absorption** – Organisms actively capture carbon during their lifecycle.  
- **Wildlife-Friendly** – Minimal light pollution supports nocturnal species.  
- **Fully Compostable** – End-of-life tiles can be safely composted or recycled.
